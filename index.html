About

I'm a final-year Physics Honours student with a Computer Science minor, working at the intersection of experimental physics, biomedical engineering, and electronics. My research spans bio-impedance spectroscopy, radiation physics, and drone systems for swarm robotics.

Beyond the lab, I've represented Sri Lanka in a climate fellowship at Vanderbilt University and engaged with U.S. Department of State officials on climate action in Washington D.C. I lead and contribute to student organisations and love translating complex ideas into clear, creative forms — whether through scientific writing, digital art, or 3D animation.

Admittance Profiling of Animal Tissues

Undergraduate Research Project · University of Peradeniya

Studying postmortem interval estimation through bio-impedance and complex admittance characterisation.
